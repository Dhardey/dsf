Our History

We're a Different Type of Service Company

Founded in 1999 in Aiken, South Carolina by John Wallace, DSF Enterprises has been delivering reliable, high-quality cleaning services to businesses and industrial plants throughout the CSRA for over 25 years. Today, we proudly remain a family-run business, with John’s daughter, Danita, joining the leadership team.

We specialize in serving commercial buildings and industrial plants, offering expert HVAC services, reliable commercial cleaning, and comprehensive preventative maintenance. Whether you need efficient heating and cooling solutions, a spotless work environment, or proactive maintenance for your equipment, we’re here to keep your operations running smoothly.
